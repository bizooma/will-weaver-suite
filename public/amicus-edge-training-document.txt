SERVICE 3: AIO SEO ANALYZER
===================================================

The AIO (AI-Optimized) Analyzer provides comprehensive website analysis
across three critical dimensions of modern search visibility.

What It Analyzes:
1. Traditional SEO: Meta tags, headings, content structure, mobile
   responsiveness, page speed, and technical SEO factors.
2. Voice SEO: How well your content is optimized for voice assistants like
   Siri, Alexa, and Google Assistant. Evaluates conversational content,
   FAQ structure, and natural language patterns.
3. AI Overview Optimization: Whether your content is structured to appear
   in AI-generated search summaries (Google AI Overviews, Bing Copilot,
   etc.). Checks schema markup, content authority, and citation-readiness.

Key Features:
- Instant website scoring across all three dimensions
- Actionable recommendations prioritized by impact
- Competitive benchmarking against other law firms
- Historical tracking to measure improvement over time
- Detailed reports exportable for client presentations

Why It Matters for Law Firms:
The digital landscape is evolving rapidly. Traditional SEO alone is no
longer sufficient. AI-powered search, voice assistants, and AI overviews
are changing how potential clients find legal services. Our analyzer
ensures your firm stays visible across all modern search channels.
